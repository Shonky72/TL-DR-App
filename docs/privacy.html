TL;DR Bot

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 2026

What we collect

TL;DR Bot stores the minimum data needed to function:

  • Your Discord user ID — to associate your settings with your account.
  • Your AI API key — provided by you via /setup. This is used solely to call your chosen AI provider when you run /tldr.
  • Channel last-checked timestamps — so the bot knows how far back to look when you run /tldr in a channel.

We do not store message content. Discord messages are fetched on demand, summarised transiently, and never written to disk.

How data is stored

All data is stored in a private, encrypted volume on Fly.io infrastructure. It is never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party other than the AI provider you chose when you ran /setup.

Your API key is sent directly to your chosen provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.) when generating a summary. TL;DR Bot does not log or inspect the content of API responses.

How to delete your data

Run /deletedata in any server where TL;DR Bot is present. This immediately and permanently removes your API key and all stored timestamps. You can verify by running /tldr — the bot will ask you to run /setup again.

Third-party services

When you run /tldr, channel messages are sent to the AI provider associated with your API key. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen provider:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
